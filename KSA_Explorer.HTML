jeddah

Jeddah is a city located on the west coast of Saudi Arabia, overlooking the Red Sea. It is the second-largest city in Saudi Arabia after Riyadh. Jeddah is famous for its beautiful beaches, historic architecture, and vibrant culture. The city is a commercial hub and is home to the country's largest port , making it an important gateway for international trade.

Riyadh

Riyadh is the capital city of Saudi Arabia and is located in the central region of the country. It is the largest city in Saudi Arabia and is the political and financial center of the country. Riyadh has a rich history and is home to many historic sites, including the famous Masmak Fortress. The city is also known for its modern architecture, luxurious shopping malls, and vibrant nightlife.

Jubail

Jubail is a city located in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia, on the coast of the Persian Gulf. It is a major industrial center and home to the world's largest petrochemical complex. Jubail is a rapidly growing city and has a diverse population. The city is known for its beautiful beaches, parks, and nature reserves, making it a popular tourist destination.
